01 · Research Design

One Question. Three Markets.

Core Research Question

What regulatory, institutional, and political economy barriers do ICT firms face when entering high-growth emerging markets — and what policy interventions can lower these barriers while advancing host countries' digital development goals?

🇮🇩
Indonesia
5G Spectrum Reform
Largest SEA digital economy. 5G mid-band clearance delayed to 2027. Spectrum fees exceed 12% of operator revenue — highest in APAC. GSMA estimates $14B economic loss from mis-pricing.
🇻🇳
Vietnam
Data Localisation
Three overlapping data laws effective July 2026: Cybersecurity Law, Data Law, Personal Data Protection Law — one of Asia's most complex compliance environments for foreign ICT operators.
🇸🇦
Saudi Arabia
Vision 2030
Four parallel regulators: CITC, SDAIA, NCA, PDPL. Government procurement demands significant local content. Geopolitical positioning shapes tech partner selection beyond formal rules.
Regulatory Architecture
Spectrum licensing, data governance, security review
Institutional Actors
Government agencies, regulators, industry associations
Political Economy
Industrial policy, geopolitical pressures, procurement bias
Policy Gaps
Misalignments between regulation and ICT firm needs
